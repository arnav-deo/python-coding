Chapter 1: Basics (Print, Variables, Input)
🟢 Easy to Medium
Write a program to print:
Hello, my name is ______

Create a variable name and store your name in it. Print:
My name is <name>

Create two variables: 
city 
country
Print them in one sentence. 

Take your age as input and print:
I am <age> years old

Take two inputs:
your name 
your favorite color
Print: 
My name is <name> and my favorite color is <color>
